## Key Innovations

1. **Credibility Scoring System (1-5 scale)**
   - 5/5: Official government documents, verified science
   - 4/5: Credible journalism, named sources
   - 3/5: Anonymous sources, unverified claims
   - 2/5: Contested sources, poorly documented
   - 1/5: Speculation, no evidence

2. **Evidence Strength Assessment**
   - Strong (5/5): Direct documentary evidence
   - Moderate (3/5): Circumstantial documentation
   - Weak (1/5): Temporal correlation only

3. **Causal Relationship Types**
   - CAUSES ‚Üí Direct causal with evidence
   - ENABLES ‚Üí Creates conditions
   - RESPONDS_TO ‚Üí Direct response
   - CORRELATES_WITH ‚Üí Temporal correlation
   - CONTRADICTS ‚Üí Conflicts with prior
